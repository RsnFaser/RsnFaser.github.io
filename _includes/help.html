Instructions To Play

Please read the instructions below before carrying on!

Use the arrow keys ↑ → ↓ ← to move around the profile exchange map.

Alternatively use the mouse to move around the map by clicking.

Click on the menu icons in the bottom corner to view information.

Alternatively click on the houses/guards on the map to view each information.

Click on the tabs to view the information inside each house box.

Click on the menu orb icons to view my social media information or this help page.
